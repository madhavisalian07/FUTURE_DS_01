# Task 1: Retail Sales Performance Analysis

## Key Insights:
* **Top Performing Category:** Technology products drive the highest revenue and profit margins compared to Furniture and Office Supplies.
* **Seasonal Peak:** Sales show a massive upward spike during the end-of-year holiday season (November and December).
* **Regional Drivers:** The West and East regions contribute to over 60% of the total profit, while certain states in the Central region are underperforming.

## Business Recommendations:
* Increase inventory and marketing budgets for high-margin Technology items right before the Q4 holiday rush.
* Launch targeted promotional campaigns in the Central region to tackle low-performing zones and clear stagnant Furniture stock.
